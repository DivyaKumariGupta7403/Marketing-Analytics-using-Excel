# Marketing-Analytics-using-Excel

Sales Performace Dashboard  
This dashboard provides a consolidated view of sales, revenue, profit, and rep/category performance across regions and quarters. It helps stakeholders identify trends, optimize strategy, and drive data-backed decisions.

Insights Obtained - 
1. Quarterly Sales & Units Trend
2. Revenue Contribution by Product Category
3. Profitability Across Categories
4. Region-wise Sales Performance
5. Sales Representative Performance
6. Average Units & Sales per Transaction

Data Driven business decisions- 
Strategic Planning -  Identifies which products to push, discontinue, or reposition.
                      Helps forecast demand and plan production/inventory level
Revenue & Profit Optimization -  Highlights high-revenue vs high-profit categories â€” guiding pricing and discount decisions.
                                 Detects low-performing areas needing intervention.				
Sales Team Performance Management -  Provides clarity on individual rep performance.
                                     Helps design fair and data-driven incentive structure.
Regional Growth Strategy - Shows where business is strong and where expansion is required.
                           Enables targeted marketing and region-specific product mixes.
Customer Behaviour Insights -  Transaction trends support cross-selling, upselling, and customer retention planning.
