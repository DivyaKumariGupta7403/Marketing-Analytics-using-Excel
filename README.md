Sales Performace Dashboard  
This dashboard provides a consolidated view of sales, revenue, profit, and rep/category performance across regions and quarters. It helps stakeholders identify trends, optimize strategy, and drive data-backed decisions.

Objective-

1.To track overall business performance across sales, revenue, and profit.

2.To identify top and low-performing products, regions, and sales reps.

3.To help managers make better decisions using clear, visual insights.

4.To understand quarterly trends and customer buying patterns for planning.

<img width="2573" height="1509" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/845d87fc-5c17-4386-a530-428628952948" />

Insights Obtained - 
1. Quarterly Sales & Units Trend
2. Revenue Contribution by Product Category
3. Profitability Across Categories
4. Region-wise Sales Performance
5. Sales Representative Performance
6. Average Units & Sales per Transaction

Data Driven business decisions- 

1.Strategic Planning -  Identifies which products to push, discontinue, or reposition.
                      Helps forecast demand and plan production/inventory level
					  
2.Revenue & Profit Optimization -  Highlights high-revenue vs high-profit categories — guiding pricing and discount decisions.
                                 Detects low-performing areas needing intervention.		
								 
3.Sales Team Performance Management -  Provides clarity on individual rep performance.
                                     Helps design fair and data-driven incentive structure.
									 
4.Regional Growth Strategy - Shows where business is strong and where expansion is required.
                           Enables targeted marketing and region-specific product mixes.
						   
5.Customer Behaviour Insights -  Transaction trends support cross-selling, upselling, and customer retention planning.

Conclusion - 

The Business Performance Dashboard provides critical insights into sales, revenue, and profitability, helping stakeholders make informed and timely decisions. 
Overall, the dashboard highlights clear opportunities for optimizing product strategy, improving regional focus, enhancing sales-force effectiveness, and boosting profitability. It serves as a powerful tool for business decision-makers to monitor performance, identify bottlenecks, and drive growth.
